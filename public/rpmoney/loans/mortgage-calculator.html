Mortgage Calculator


Not sure if you can afford a home loan? Use calculator to work out what your regular payments would be, as well as the total repayment and amount of interest. Try changing the term length to see how the overall interest amount is affected. By reducing the length of the loan you can make big savings on interest costs.

Tip: If you use a 'deposit' of $0, you can also use this calculator for personal and auto loans
